Swimming with whale sharks

2017-01-13

Derawan Island (Google Maps)

Located just off the east coast of Kalimantan in the Berau regency.

All tours offer pretty much the same 3-night package listed below:

  • 4 days 3 nights
  • accommodation (can be upgraded to others available on the island)
  • all meals
  • speedboats during stay (boats and other transport to main harbor and airport not included)
  • snorkeling gear
  • tour guide
  • visit to:
    • Derawan Island
    • Maratua Island
    • Kakaban, jellyfish lake
    • Manta ray spot
    • Talisayan, whale shark swim spot
    • Senggalau Gusung Island
    • Goa Haji Mangku and Laguna Kehe Daing (if the water level is safe)
    • Labuan Cermin
